Everything you need to track spending
No subscriptions. No cloud required. Everything stays on your iPhone.
📷

Instant OCR Scanning

VisionKit + Vision framework reads Italian and English receipts with high accuracy. Always shows an editable preview so you can fix any errors.

🏷️

Smart Categorization

Keyword rules automatically assign categories. Correct once — Slippy learns and remembers your preferences for future receipts.

📊

Monthly Dashboard

See your total spend, % change vs last month, and a colour-coded bar chart per category. Navigate any month in history.

✨

AI Saving Tips

Powered by Claude. Get a short, specific, actionable suggestion based on what you actually spent. Works offline gracefully.

🔒

Private by Default

All data lives in Core Data on your device. API key stored in iOS Keychain. Zero telemetry, zero analytics.

📤

CSV Export

Export all receipts as a spreadsheet-ready CSV via the iOS share sheet. Perfect for expense reports or personal budgeting.
